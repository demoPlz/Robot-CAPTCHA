TITLE: "Drawer" Task Hints
The plastic plate attached to the middle drawer is a handle for the robot to grasp.
<br>
The progression of the task: 1. if drawer isn't open and object is on the desk, open the drawer; 
2. If the drawer is opened, venture to grasp the object, and put object into the drawer; 
3. After putting the object into the drawer, close the drawer.
